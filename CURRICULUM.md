# 🎓 BSc in Information Technology – University of Gondar

**Program:** Bachelor of Science in Information Technology  
**University:** University of Gondar, Ethiopia 🇪🇹  
**Duration:** 4 Years (8 Semesters)  
**Status:** Currently in Year 3  

---

## 📌 Program Overview
The curriculum is designed to produce graduates with strong practical skills in managing, configuring, and maintaining ICT infrastructures. It emphasizes problem-solving, entrepreneurship, and the ability to handle an organization’s technology needs – from troubleshooting to system integration and administration.

## 🎯 Graduate Profile
Graduates will be able to:
- Analyze and define IT requirements for organizations.
- Identify and evaluate emerging technologies.
- Design, implement, and administer IT-based solutions.
- Demonstrate best practices, standards, and ethical conduct.
- Work collaboratively in teams and communicate effectively.
